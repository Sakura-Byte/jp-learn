JPL Japanese Language and Culture Summer Course

日本語と文化のサマーコース

We are looking forward to meeting many Japan enthusiasts. Join us for an immersive Japanese language and cultural experience!

Goal

We transform your existing knowledge into real-world skills using practical and easy-to-understand learning materials.

Level & Textbook

Level: JPL002 or equivalent

Textbook: "Irodori: Japanese for Life"

  • Practical textbook for foreigners living in Japan
  • Provides insight into Japanese daily life
  • Useful for travel in Japan
  • Helps you converse with Japanese friends

Who Should Join?

Practice Japanese Travel to Japan Enjoy Conversations

Methodology

Three native Japanese-speaking instructors will speak to you in Japanese as much as possible.

You will be immersed in Japanese for 30 hours over 10 days.

This is not "studying" but "practice." Learn communication skills by understanding language in context through situations, expressions, and gestures.

Change your mindset!
