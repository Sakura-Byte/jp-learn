Methodology

Three native Japanese-speaking instructors will speak to you in Japanese as much as possible.

You will be immersed in Japanese for 30 hours over 10 days.

This is not "studying" but "practice." Learn communication skills by understanding language in context through situations, expressions, and gestures.

Change your mindset!
